✓ 1-Day Fast

Best for: Beginners, weekly resets, autophagy boost

✓ 2-Day Fast

Best for: Intermediate fasters, deeper autophagy, cellular repair

Health & Wellness During Fasting

⚡

Electrolytes

Sodium, potassium, and magnesium regulate muscle function and hydration balance. Especially important on fasts longer than 24 hours.

💡 Tip: Add electrolyte powder to water or take mineral supplements.

🪨

Minerals

Magnesium, calcium, and zinc support bone health, immune function, and energy production during extended fasts.

💡 Tip: Consider a mineral supplement on days 2+.

🥗

Vitamins

Take your multivitamins during eating windows to maintain nutrient stores and support immune health.

💡 Tip: B-complex vitamins aid energy metabolism.

💧

Hydration First

Pure water is your primary tool. Aim to drink regularly throughout your fasting period to manage hunger and support detoxification.

💡 Tip: Sip slowly; large amounts at once can cause bloating.

⚠️ Important Safety Guidelines

  • Stop fasting immediately if you experience severe dizziness, chest pain, or shortness of breath.
  • People with eating disorders, diabetes, or on medications should consult a doctor before fasting.
  • Fasts longer than 3 days require medical supervision for most people.
  • Pregnant and breastfeeding individuals should not fast without medical approval.
  • Never fast if you're ill or recovering from illness.
